# FitMetric Pro - Product Features & Documentation

FitMetric Pro is a high-performance, AI-integrated health and weight tracking suite designed for precision health management.

## 1. Authentication & User Management
- **Secure Onboarding**: Dedicated landing page highlighting core value propositions.
- **Identity Management**: Sign-up and Login system with local data isolation per user.
- **Profile Isolation**: Data is automatically siloed so multiple users can track progress on the same device.
- **Session Control**: Logout functionality to secure private health data.

## 2. Core Tracking Modules
- **Weight Tracking**: 
    - Log morning and evening weights to capture circadian fluctuations.
    - Visual indicators showing delta from the target goal.
    - Date-specific logging with optional notes.
- **Body Measurements**:
    - Track Waist, Chest, Hips, Thighs, Arms, and Neck.
    - Essential for tracking "Non-Scale Victories" (NSVs) during body recomposition.
- **Hydration Tracking**:
    - Quick-add logs for water intake.
    - Daily goal visualization and progress tracking.

## 3. AI-Powered Nutrition
- **Vision Scanner**: Use the device camera to scan nutritional labels. Gemini AI extracts macros and calories automatically.
- **Natural Language Food Search**: Describe a meal (e.g., "Two eggs and a slice of avocado toast") and let AI estimate the nutritional breakdown.
- **Manual Logging**: Full control to input specific calories, protein, carbs, and fats.
- **Smart Meal Prep**:
    - AI generates a personalized 7-day meal plan based on user goals (lose/gain).
    - Automatic Shopping List generation categorized by aisle.

## 4. Medication & Supplement Protocol
- **Stack Management**: Add vitamins, medications, or supplements with specific dosages and purposes.
- **Daily Checklist**: Interactive daily protocol to ensure 100% adherence.
- **History Tracking**: Log completion of medication tasks alongside health metrics.

## 5. Intelligence & Analytics
- **AI Health Assistant**: A dedicated chat interface with Gemini 3 Flash. It has context of your recent logs and uses Google Search to provide grounded, scientific advice.
- **Health Insights**: Automated analysis of weight trends and nutritional balance using Google Search grounding to reference current health standards.
- **Interactive Analytics**: 
    - Dynamic SVG weight evolution charts.
    - Performance metrics: Net change, weekly pace, and target delta.
- **Achievements**: Gamified badge system for consistency streaks in weighing and logging.

## 6. Pro Features & Membership
- **Premium Tier**: Gated access to AI Assistant and advanced analytics.
- **Dynamic Nutrition Planning**: Auto-calibrating calorie targets based on the user's current weight and weekly pace goals.
- **Medical Disclaimer**: Integrated safety warnings and professional guidance notices.
